The Big Bang Theory

is a popular American sitcom created by Chuck Lorre and Bill Prady. The series primarily revolves around the lives of four socially awkward scientists - Sheldon Cooper, Leonard Hofstadter, Howard Wolowitz, and Rajesh Koothrappali - and their interactions with their neighbor, aspiring actress Penny. The show explores their friendships, romantic relationships, and their humorous misadventures in both their personal and professional lives. It often incorporates elements of geek culture, science, and pop culture references, making it appealing to a wide audience.
